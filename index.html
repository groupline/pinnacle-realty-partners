Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about selling your house for cash

How do you determine your offer price?

+

We analyze recent comparable sales, current market conditions, and your property's condition to make a fair cash offer. Our goal is to provide a win-win solution that works for both parties.

Are there any fees or commissions?

+

No fees, no commissions, no closing costs. The offer we make is the amount you'll receive at closing. We cover all closing costs and there are no hidden fees.

Do I have to accept your offer?

+

Absolutely not! Our offers are completely no-obligation. Take your time to review it and decide if it's right for you. There's no pressure whatsoever.

What types of properties do you buy?

+

We buy all types of residential properties: single-family homes, condos, townhomes, duplexes, and small apartment buildings. We buy in any condition, any situation.
